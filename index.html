Founder's Story

The Practitioner
Behind the Vision

Sharad Somani doesn't teach leadership from a textbook. He has lived it—steering complex infrastructure projects across fourteen countries, navigating boardroom dynamics at the highest levels, and witnessing first-hand what separates leaders who endure from those who merely survive.

At KPMG Singapore, Sharad leads one of the region's most consequential advisory practices—infrastructure, energy transition, and ESG consulting—where every engagement demands clarity under pressure, strategic precision, and the ability to unite diverse stakeholders around a common vision. That experience became the foundation for APEX.

But Sharad's philosophy of leadership extends beyond boardrooms. Having seen the toll that relentless high performance takes on even the most capable executives, he became equally committed to the question of sustainability: how do great leaders continue to lead brilliantly—for decades, not quarters?

"The most demanding clients teach you the best. When you deliver successfully, you earn their trust and forge life-long partnerships. True character comes out when you stand with your client and say, 'Let's ride through this together.'"

APEX is the synthesis of both answers: rigorous, evidence-based executive coaching through Apex Coaching, and nature-immersive, holistic renewal through Apex Thrive. Together they form a complete system for leaders who refuse to choose between performance and wellbeing.
